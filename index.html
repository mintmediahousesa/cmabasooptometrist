About Dr C Mabaso

Professional care, grounded in community.

Dr C Mabaso is an experienced optometrist committed to providing accessible, patient-focused eye care to the Phalaborwa community. His professional background spans optometry, nutrition, business leadership and continued clinical development, giving patients a rounded approach to eye health and everyday visual comfort.

The practice focuses on thorough eye examinations, eyewear guidance, contact lens support and long-term patient relationships. Every consultation is approached with care, clarity and respect, helping individuals and families make confident decisions about their vision.
